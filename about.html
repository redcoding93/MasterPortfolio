About Me

I believe that everyone's voices should be heard and that technology can be used to make a positive impact on society and can serve as a platform to facilitate the diverse voice and will of the people. I have attended various tech workshops, speakers, and networking events, and I am a dedicated independent programmer who is constantly reading and learning about new programming languages and tech advancements.
